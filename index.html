Quick answers
Do I own the system?
Yes. You own the code, the design, everything. No monthly fees. Just hosting (₦5K–₦20K/month).
How long does it take?
2–6 weeks depending on complexity. Discovery call → Proposal → Build (weekly check-ins) → Launch with 30-day support.
What happens after I pay?
We start with a discovery call to map out exactly what you need. Then I build with weekly check-ins. When it's ready, we do a handoff call where I walk you through everything. You get 30 days of direct support after launch.
Can I add more systems later?
Absolutely. That's the point. Start with what you need most, then add components as you grow. Everything is built to work together.
How is this different from WordPress/Squarespace?
Templates force you to fit your business into someone else's idea of how things should work. I build custom systems that match your actual workflow. No plugins to update, no templates to fight.
I'm not technical. Will I be able to use it?
Yes. I build for creatives, not developers. You get training and documentation. If you can use Instagram, you can use these systems.
